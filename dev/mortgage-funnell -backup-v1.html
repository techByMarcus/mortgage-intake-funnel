Pricing Guarantee — Overview

  • Requires a complete loan application, including credit authorization and supporting documentation
  • Must be based on a written, current, and comparable loan offer
  • Comparison must reflect the same borrower profile, loan type, property, occupancy, and overall loan structure
  • The comparison must be based on a current credit profile within the same 30-day credit window
  • Expired, outdated, or materially different scenarios are not eligible
  • Locked and non-locked pricing must be compared on a like-for-like basis
  • Competing offers must be current and verifiable at the time of review
  • Not all scenarios or loan types qualify
  • Additional terms, conditions, and limitations may apply

Full details can be reviewed during the application process.
